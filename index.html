Silicon Valley

Silicon Valley is an American comedy television series created by Mike Judge for HBO which portrays the lives of a group of young software programmers and developers trying to "make it big" in the technology Mecca of California's Silicon Valley. The series premiered April 6, 2014.

Silicon Valley basically revolves around a group of "techie" friends who aspire to build a startup in Silicon Valley using a brilliant idea for an App (mobile application). The story revolves around their journey of being amateur entrepreneurs.
